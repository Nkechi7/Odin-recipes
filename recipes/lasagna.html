Lasagna

← Back to Home

Description

This lasagna is rich, cheesy, and packed with flavor!

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef
  • Tomato sauce
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Boil noodles
  2. Cook the beef
  3. Layer ingredients
  4. Bake at 375°F (190°C) for 45 mins
